Kira the Maleficent

Hello, my name is Kira and I'm a maleficent cat. I live in a nice house, with two humans and another cat named Hoshi. They all exist just to please me!

Here are some things I like to do:

  • Sleep.
  • Do mean things to Hoshi!
  • Be petted by my favourite human.

And some things I hate:

  • To see Hoshi be petted. Whenever this happens he feels my revenge afterwards.
  • To be kissed in the belly by the second human.
  • Children!
